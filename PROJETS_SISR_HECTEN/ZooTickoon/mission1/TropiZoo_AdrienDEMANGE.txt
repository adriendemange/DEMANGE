Theme and Name of the Zoo
Theme: Tropical Wildlife Adventure
Name: TropiZoo
TropiZoo is a tropical-themed zoo, highlighting the beauty and diversity of species from tropical regions across the globe. The experience is immersive, bringing visitors into lush environments filled with exotic plants and animals.

2. Categories of Services or Products
Category 1: Food & Beverages

Tropical Fruit Smoothies: Freshly blended smoothies made from tropical fruits like mango, pineapple, and guava.
Jungle Café: A sit-down restaurant offering meals inspired by tropical cuisines (e.g., Caribbean jerk chicken, Thai curries).
Safari Snack Bars: Pop-up snack stands with quick bites like plantain chips, tropical fruit salads, and coconut water.
Category 2: Educational Experiences

Guided Eco Tours: Led by expert guides, these tours take visitors through various habitats, explaining wildlife behavior and conservation efforts.
Wildlife Encounters: Scheduled interactions with animals like toucans, sloths, and tropical reptiles where visitors can learn about them up close.
Conservation Workshops: Hands-on learning experiences about environmental conservation, biodiversity, and the zoo’s efforts in protecting endangered species.
Category 3: Retail & Souvenirs

TropiGift Shop: Featuring eco-friendly souvenirs, stuffed animals, and toys themed around tropical wildlife.
Artisan Craft Market: A section of the zoo dedicated to selling handmade crafts from tropical regions, like jewelry, pottery, and textiles.
Adopt an Animal Program: Visitors can "adopt" an animal to support the zoo’s conservation efforts and receive an adoption certificate, plush toy, and quarterly updates on their animal.
3. Colour Codes, Typography, and Logo
Colour Codes:

Primary Color: #00A36C (Tropical Green) – Represents the lush vegetation of the tropics.
Secondary Color: #FFD700 (Sunshine Yellow) – Evokes the warmth and vibrance of the sun.
Accent Color: #FF4500 (Tropical Sunset Orange) – Adds a warm contrast and tropical flare.
Complementary Color: #4682B4 (Tropical Sky Blue) – Balances the palette with a cool, relaxing tone.
These colors use a triadic scheme (green, yellow, and orange) complemented by a touch of blue for balance.

Typography:

Headings: Montserrat Bold
Body Text: Lato Regular
These fonts are modern, clean, and easy to read, evoking both professionalism and the playful nature of a zoo.
